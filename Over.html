ABOUT ME

Nick Stoker, a passionate filmmaker, dedicated his life to storytelling through film. He is fueled by an unwavering passion for filmmaking, skillfully transformed stories into vivid realities through his camera lens. 

With each project, he continued to push boundaries, leaving a lasting impact on cinema.It all started at a young age where nick got his first camera and turned the dullest moments into something brilliant. This is where nick discovered his passion. 

Since that moment he developedhimself into a film maker to be proud of.
